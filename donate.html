Sponsor for a Banner or Specific Features

Companies as well as individuals who'd like extra exposure or who want to push for specific features to get added can sponsor Tiled at the following tiers:

  • $100 / month - Banner Sponsor - Get a banner link on Tiled's front page!
  • $200 / month - Feature Sponsor - Get 4 hours/month of dedicated development time!

The above tiers can be combined if desired. Please choose your tier on either GitHub Sponsors (recommended for non-EU sponsors) or Patreon (EU sponsors)

There is also the option of contracting Thorbjørn Lindeijer to add to Tiled whatever features you need!
